About Our Community

An Established Community Right on the Shore

Ocean Tower is a residential condominium association located steps from the Atlantic Ocean in Ocean City, Maryland.

The Building

Comfortable Living, Steps from the Atlantic

Ocean Tower is a well-established condominium community situated at 73rd Street in Ocean City, Maryland — one of the most desirable locations on the coast.

Our association is committed to maintaining a safe, clean, and enjoyable environment for all residents and owners through proactive management and community-driven improvements.
